MuddMath is our (roughly) annual newsletter about our department, mathematical activities at Mudd, and the activities of our alumni.

Contribute!

We welcome letters, updates, and news items from and about our alumni via e-mail (muddmath at math.hmc.edu) or snail mail (c/o MuddMath, Department of Mathematics, Harvey Mudd College, 301 Platt Blvd, Claremont, CA 91711.)

We love to hear from you!

Subscribe!

We probably have your contact information if you're keeping the alumni office up to date. But if you're not and you'd like to get on the list, please write to us and let us know your address (as well as what you've been up to!).
